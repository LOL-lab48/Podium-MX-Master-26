# Podium-MX-Master-26
**Podium MX Master 26** is a deep, text-based motocross team management simulator where strategy, risk, and reputation decide your path to the top.

Build and manage your own motocross team across full race seasons, signing riders through strategic contract negotiations where every decision matters. Find the perfect deal to secure top talent before rival teams steal them, balancing salary, bonuses, contract length, and timing to hit each rider’s hidden “sweet spot.”

Compete across dynamic race calendars with detailed simulations influenced by rider skill, bike performance, track conditions, and unpredictable events. Every race delivers full results and immersive recaps featuring crashes, breakthroughs, and pivotal moments that shape your season.

Grow your reputation through performance, media decisions, and smart management to attract better sponsors, boost merch income, and unlock stronger riders. Manage finances carefully — overspending, poor results, or bad press can lead to losing sponsor trust, board support, or even your job.

With AI-controlled rival teams, evolving storylines, a living news system, and no reliance on visuals, Podium MX Master 26 focuses purely on decision-making, strategy, and the thrill of building a championship-winning motocross empire.
